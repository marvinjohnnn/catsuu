About Us

Heartwork School of Music & Arts provides a family-friendly, supportive environment. Our instructors are professional music educators, many of whom also perform at local and international venues.

A musical education provides children with a means of self-expression. Questions about the arts do not have only one right answer, a study of the arts provides children with an internal glimpse of other cultures and teaches them to be empathetic towards other people. This development of compassion and empathy provides a bridge across cultural chasms that leads to respect of other races at an early age.

Our Mission

Heartwork’s mission is to create, provide and expand positively life-changing experiences through music and arts education for all. We believe that music and arts education is a core to students’ education and must be available to all students. Heartwork is an advocate for music and arts education.

Our Vision

Heartwork will be a catalyst to ensure that every child has access and opportunity to participate in active music-making in his or her scholastic environment.

We will use our resources to provide local programs that recognize and support our students' performance and success as well as assisting them in supporting by promoting awareness of music and arts impact on student growth and achievement.

Services offered

Music Lessons

Our mission is to strengthen communities through music education and we welcome anyone who wishes to study music. We offer private music lessons in a wide variety of instruments and styles, plus workshops, classes, and ensembles.

Art Lessons

Artistic expression has been a constant element throughout human history. Since the first cave paintings, artistic styles have been evolving and developing with the human race. In some cases artworks give us the clearest understanding of what life was like at different points in history. Let’s make history. Apply for our art classes.

Let's Move

Let's move. Express yourself. Great for mind and body. Dance classes will help dancers with posture, flexibility, fitness, balance, self-discipline, and self-confidence.

Multimedia Lessons

Numerous jobs are waiting for multimedia artists around the world. Students will gain an understanding of new media. Explore technical and artistic ways to produce digital imagery and create interactive multimedia projects
